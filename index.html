Mood Memo

As one of my initial independent projects on Xcode using SwiftUI, I found myself eager to craft something practical and well-suited for daily use. Given the popularity of journaling and lifestyle tracking, I decided to take my first steps into this realm.

The project is currently tailored for iOS 16.4, but I have plans to enhance its capabilities for iOS 17. This will involve integrating widgets to seamlessly interact with the core features of the application.

Users can assign colors or 'Moods' to calendar days, along with writing memos stored by date. Editing is intuitive via quick actions or opening an edit sheet.

This app is a work in progress, with frameworks for mood editing and default view switching still being built. Sticking with the idea that dedicated memo and mood editing views will enhance interface navigation, I plan to implement those as well.
